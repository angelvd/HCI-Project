    • Characters

    Crystal Maiden

    Abilities:

    • Crystal Nova
      • A burst of damaging frost slows enemy movement and attack rate in the targeted area.
    • Frostbite
      • Encases an enemy unit in ice, prohibiting movement and attack, while dealing damage over time.
    • Arcane Aura
      • Gives additional mana regeneration to all friendly units on the map.
    • Freezing Field
      • Surrounds Crystal Maiden with random icy explosions that slow enemies and deal massive damage.

    Dawnbreaker

    Abilities:

    • Starbreaker
      • Whirls her hammer around 3 times, damaging enemies with her attack plus bonus damage.
    • Celestial Hammer
      • Hurls her weapon at a target, damaging enemies struck along the way. Leaving a trail of fire that slows enemies.
    • Luminosity
      • A critical hit that heals all allied heroes within 650 units around her for a portion of attack damage dealt.
    • Solar Guardian
      • Creates a pulsing effect at a location within 350 units of an allied hero anywhere on the map, damaging enemies and healing allies with each pulse.

    Enchantress

    Abilities:

    • Impetus
      • Places an enchantment on each attack while activated, causing it to deal additional damage based on how far away the target is. The farther the target, the greater the damage dealt.
    • Enchant
      • Enchantress charms an enemy. If the enemy is a hero, they are slowed. If the enemy is a creep, she brings it under her control, increasing it's attack damage and max health.
    • Nature's Attendants
      • A cloud of wisps heals Enchantress and any friendly units nearby. Lasts 12.0 seconds.
    • Untouchable
      • Beguiles her enemies, slowing their attacks when she is attacked.

    Queen of Pain

    Abilities:

    • Shadow Strike
      • Hurls a poisoned dagger which deals large initial damage, and then deals damage over time.
    • Blink
      • Short distance teleportation that allows Queen of Pain to move in and out of combat.
    • Scream of Pain
      • The Queen of Pain lets loose a piercing scream around her, damaging nearby enemies.
    • Sonic Wave
      • Creates a gigantic wave of sound in front of Queen of Pain, dealing heavy damage to all enemy units in its wake and pushing them back.

    Teamplar Assasin

    Abilities:

    • Refaction
      • Templar Assassin becomes highly elusive, avoiding damage and gaining a bonus to her damage.
    • Meld
      • Templar Assassin conceals herself, becoming invisible as long as she remains still.
    • "PSI Blades
      • Slice through the attacked unit, splitting and damaging enemy units directly behind it, while gaining bonus attack range.
    • Psionic Projection
      • Allows Templar Assassin to teleport to any Psionic Trap after a brief channel. Detonates the Psionic Trap on arrival.

    Windranger

    Abilities:

    • Shackleshot
      • Shackles the target to an enemy unit or tree in a line directly behind it.
    • Powershot
      • Windranger charges her bow for up to 1.0 second for a single powerful shot, which deals more damage the longer it is charged. The arrow damages enemies along its path.
    • Windrun
      • Increases movement speed and adds evasion from all physical attacks, while slowing movement of nearby enemies.
    • Focus Fire
      • Windranger channels the wind to gain 475 additional attack speed against a single enemy unit or structure, though with a reduction to her attack damage.
